Steak Dinner

Here we have a beautiful steak dinner, A personal favourite from Cian Healy

Ingredients

  • Good cut of meat
  • Seasoning including salt and pepper
  • Well oiled hot pan

Steps

  1. Leave your cut of meat out to come to room temperature
  2. ensure steak is well marinated with salt and pepper on both sides
  3. place steak on searing hot pan
  4. cook each side for 3-4 mins to achieve medium
  5. remove meat off the pan and let it rest before serving
